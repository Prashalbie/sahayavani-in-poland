Built by the community,
for the community

Sahayavani (सहायवाणी — "voice of help") was started by Indians who spent too much time searching Facebook groups, WhatsApp threads, and outdated blogs for answers that should have been easy to find.

Everything here is free. No ads. No logins. No data collected. Just accurate, community-verified information — updated every week.

⚠️ This site is maintained by the community. Always verify critical information with official Polish government sources.
